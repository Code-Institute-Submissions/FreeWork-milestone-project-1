About FreeWork

Founded in 2009 we are a 100% Irish-owned company.

Winners of the Innovative Business Award for the years: 2011, 2014, 2015.

We are experiencing rapid growth. We now have offices in Cork, Ballincollig, Galway and Dublin to meet your growing demand.

We are committed to providing a cutting edge professional environment and bespoke services to our residents and visitors.
