About FreeWork

We offer flexible office and workspace solutions to SMEs and individuals. We have a number of packages available ranging from flexible hotdesking, to resident desks and dedicated office spaces for small teams.

We are experiencing rapid growth. We now have spaces in Cork, Galway and Dublin to meet your growing demand.

Winners of the Innovative Business Award for the years: 2011, 2014, 2015.

Founded in 2009 we are a 100% Irish-owned company.

Our Spaces

The Planting Room

Our beautiful Planting Room has a lush green design. With hanging and potted plants throughout the space this is a calming and tranquil space that is sure to get you in a focused, creative mood. Our Planting Room has options and spaces for hotdesking and permanent deskspaces so for small enterprises and individuals this could be a perfect fit.

The Halls

The Halls is home to our dedicated office spaces. Offering a flexible home to small and medium sized teams these tasteful and relaxed spaces offer a focused environment for you and your business to thrive in. Get in contact to get a quote on our three different office sizes.

The Lounge

The Lounge is our most laid back and casual space. With comfortable couches and a state of the art coffee station/kitchenette the Lounge expresses comfort, convenience and a more social atmosphere. Whether you want to pop in for a coffee, set up and work from one of the couches or just pop in for a chat to refuel your creative juices the Lounge is a very positive and energetic space. Access to the Lounge is included with all Packages and Plans.
